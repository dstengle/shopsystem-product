**Decision-doc coherence model ruled and drafted.** Diagnosing how to
formalize PDR-034's relationship to PDR-032, the product authority ruled a
general model for decision documents: **no amendments — full-supersede
copy-forward** (a changed doc is replaced by a new self-contained doc that
copies surviving content forward and fully supersedes the old, so the
`status` field never lies — the IETF "Obsoletes" model), and **keep decisions
in-lane** (PDR = product/what/who, ADR = architecture/how; cross-lane
relationships are references, never amendments). Scope: decision docs
(ADR/PDR/brief); scenarios are excluded because they are cited by stable
`@scenario_hash` identity and keep the stable-identity amendment model.

partially-refined by ADR-059, carried a now-false appetite line, and a

`accepted` doc.
